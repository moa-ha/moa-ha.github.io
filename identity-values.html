Identity, Values and Strengths Exploration

Identity
1. Honesty COURAGE; Speaking the truth but more broadly presenting oneself in a genuine way and acting in a sincere way; being without pretense; taking responsibility for one's feelings and actions.
Values
2. Judgment WISDOM; Thinking things through and examining them from all sides; not jumping to conclusions; being able to change one's mind in light of evidence; weighing all evidence fairly.
Strengths
3. Humility TEMPERANCE; Letting one's accomplishments speak for themselves; not regarding oneself as more special than one is.
